Why the roof matters first

By securing the roof now, we help protect the theatre hall, the first-floor salon, and the wider heritage of Casino Mayor for the future.

  • Protect the building from further weather damage.
  • Support the preservation of its historic spaces and character.
  • Follow the journey as the next phases of restoration unfold.

Here, "casino" follows the historic Spanish meaning: a social and cultural meeting house for conversation, music, dance, and community life.
