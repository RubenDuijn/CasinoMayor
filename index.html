Experiences

From the Theatre stage to mountain routes beyond the village, from social dance nights to private stays, Casino Mayor brings together culture, rhythm, and hospitality under one roof.

Dance Academy Nights

Salsa, Bachata, and Kizomba evenings across multiple rooms, with beginners, intermediate, and advanced tracks led by guest artists and local instructors.

Live Stage

Dance classes led by renowned artists, weekly concerts, Latin nights, and DJ sets — each rooted in the Theatre stage, each evening its own format.

Social Dance Night

An open evening to practice your Salsa, Bachata, Kizomba, and Tango skills in a relaxed social setting, with space for partner work, rounds, and informal coaching.

Mountain Escapes

Build a stay around the Sierra: hiking routes and bouldering by day, the village in the evening, and a return to wine, tapas, or cultural programming inside Casino Mayor.

Group Stays & Apartments

Stay in the building's apartments or shape a group stay around dance, dining, and the Sierra — a stronger hospitality offer for weekends, retreats, and private gatherings.

Upstairs Cocktail Room

A calmer first-floor room with its own cocktail bar, suited to late conversations, private bookings, and evenings that prefer atmosphere over noise.

Private Events

Flexible spaces for corporate evenings and celebrations, with integrated catering, technical setup, and host support.

Dress code and booking windows vary by event. Reservations are recommended for stage evenings, Latin nights, and peak weekends. Special seasonal programmes run around La Candelaria (2 Feb), Santa Ana (26 Jul), La Boda Típica (August), and Semana Santa.

Yearly Events

Candelario's calendar shapes our programme. These are the moments we build around each year.

2 Feb
La Candelaria

Bodega & Charcuterie Evening

The village marks the end of the chacinera season with a communal tasting of freshly made charcuterie. We celebrate with local embutido paired with wine from our own bodega, and a folk music set on the Theatre stage.

1 May
Cristo del Refugio

Heritage Spring Evening

Holy Week closes and the village's spring procession marks a new season. Casino Mayor reopens its full programme with acoustic music on the Theatre stage and a curated bodega menu.

26 Jul
(4 days)
Santa Ana — Fiestas Mayores

Gala Stage Dinner & Peak Programme

The patron saint festivities draw the largest crowd of the year. Four days of music and culture in the streets; inside Casino Mayor all rooms are in full operation, the stage hosts headline concerts, and the evening of 26 July closes with a Gala Stage Dinner. Reservations essential.

Aug
(2nd Sunday)
La Boda Típica

Heritage Gala Evening

A theatrical recreation of a 19th-century Candelario wedding — declared a Festival of Regional Tourist Interest. The village fills with visitors in traditional dress. We host a complementary evening: live music, a traditional dance exhibition on stage, and a themed bodega menu.

Mar / Apr
Semana Santa

Easter Cultural Programme

Holy Week brings cultural visitors to Candelario. Casino Mayor runs cinema screenings, acoustic sets, and a curated bodega wine tasting across the Easter break. Lighter format, earlier opening hours.
